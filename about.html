ABOUT US

Welcome to Anurag Engineering College! 
Anurag Engineering College is established in 2001 and is reputed as one of the most preferred college for engineering education within short time. The college has created a resourceful and inspiring academic ambience and world class infrastructure. The college is Private Self Financing Institution. The College is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The College is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH). TEQIP granted College. An UGC Autonomous Institution.

MECHANICAL DEPARTMENT

Mechanical Department in Anurag Engineering College is established in 2004, with a Professor and three Associate Professors and an intake of 60. Now the Department has an efficient team of 43 faculty members with a Professor, Three Associate Professors and 40 Assistant Professors, headed by Mr.K.Veeranjaneyulu M.Tech, (Ph.D). The Department has 11 labs which are fully equipped with latest quality machinery, equipment, tools and instruments, to meet the requirements of JNTUH, AUTONOMOUS syllabus and AICTE Norms. The main objective of Mechanical Engineering Association is to impart the technical and soft skills education and to develop the leadership qualities among the students of Mechanical Engg. Dept.

ADWITIYA 2020

Adwitiya-2020 is conducted by Mechanical Students Association MECHNOMICZ which belongs to the Department of Mech Adwitiya-2020. It is a oneday National Live Symposium, aims to bring cutting edge creative and technical skills of participants in various technical and cultural Events, which demands knowledge and expertise and there by brings competing talents.
