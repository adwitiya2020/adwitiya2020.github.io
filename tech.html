Working Model

Working Model (WM) is an engineering model Virtual mechanical components, such as springs, ropes, and motors are combined with objects in a 3D working space. After the model is run, the model will simulate the interaction of the model's parts and can also graph the movement and force on any element in the project. It is useful for basic physics simulations, and can be quite a powerful dynamic geometric analytical tool, once you learn it. One drawback is that the basic commands like those for pulley and rope simulations appear to be limited to the 'string through a hole' type, but such limitations are quickly overtaken using the internal formula and scripting features of the model.
WM was developed from a program called Interactive Physics, the fundamental difference between the two is that WM will read therefore being the conversion of Interactive Physics to the engineering and industrial world.

Enterpreneurship

Entrepreneurship is the process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business. The people who create these businesses are called entrepreneurs.
